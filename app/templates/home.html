Doki-Doki Encryption

Doki-Doki Literature Club is a disturbing horror game. Although it seems like a cute dating simulator at first, the game becomes crazy. Some characters leave clues on your computer.

One of these clues consists of a 800x800 image of black and white pixels. A black pixel is a binary 1, and a white pixel is a 0. The image is read from left to right, just like a book. This program converts utf-8 text into one of these images, and can decode images created in a similar way. Enjoy Doki-Doki Encryption!
